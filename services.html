Company Overview:


BT-Academy is a pioneering tech company dedicated to providing comprehensive programming training services to individuals and companies.
Founded with a mission to empower individuals with programming skills and facilitate the growth of a robust tech ecosystem in Africa, BT
-Academy aims to democratize access to programming knowledge and opportunities for economic advancement.

Services Offered:



BT-Academy offers structured and personalized programming courses designed to cater to individuals at various skill levels, from beginners to
advanced learners.
Courses cover a wide range of programming languages and technologies, including but not limited to Python, Java, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, and
more.
Training programs are delivered through online platforms, live workshops, and inperson sessions conducted by experienced instructors.

BT-Academy not only teaches programming skills but also guides individuals on leveraging these skills to create entrepreneurial opportunities.
Entrepreneurial workshops and mentorship programs equip learners with the knowledge and tools needed to start their techbased
or freelance careers.

BT-Academy offers tailored training solutions for companies seeking to upskill their workforce in programming and related tech skills.
Customized corporate training programs are designed to address specific industry needs and challenges, helping companies stay competitive in
the digital age.

Mission and Objectives:


At BTWORLD, our mission is to build a vibrant and inclusive tech community in Africa by:
Making programming education accessible to all individuals regardless of their background or prior experience.
Empowering individuals with the skills and resources needed to thrive in the digital economy.
Fostering innovation and entrepreneurship within the African tech ecosystem.
Partnering with businesses and organizations to drive technological advancements and economic growth across the continent.

Why Choose BTWORLD:


1. Comprehensive Curriculum: BT-Academy offers a diverse range of courses covering popular programming languages and technologies,
learners receive a wellrounded education.
2. Experienced Instructors: Our instructors are highly skilled professionals with extensive experience in the tech industry, providing learners with
practical insights and realworld knowledge.
3. Flexible Learning Options: Whether you prefer selfpaced online courses or interactive workshops, BT-Academy offers flexible learning options to
accommodate your schedule and learning style.
4. Entrepreneurial Focus: In addition to technical skills, BT-Academy emphasizes the importance of entrepreneurship, equipping learners with the
tools and guidance needed to succeed in the competitive tech landscape.
5. Commitment to Diversity and Inclusion: BT-Academy is committed to creating an inclusive learning environment where individuals from all
backgrounds feel welcome and supported.
At BTWORLD, we believe that everyone has the potential to become a proficient programmer and contribute to the advancement of technology.
Join us on our journey to build a brighter future for Africa through programming education and innovation.
